HOW DOES IT WORK

Revolutionize Your Voting Process: Effortless, Secure, and Transparent Online Elections

Conducting online elections is as simple as a 3-step process. With our user-friendly platform, you can seamlessly set up, conduct, and generate results for your elections, ensuring a smooth and efficient voting experience for all.

Set-up Election

Setting up your election on eVote is a breeze. In just a few simple steps, you can have your election ready to go: Add Election Details: Provide essential information such as the election date, time, candidate names, and photos. Customize your election to reflect your unique requirements. Add Voter List: Import your voter list with ease using our convenient spreadsheet template. Simply drag and drop the list, and our system will take care of the rest. Review & Launch: Take a moment to review all the entered details, ensuring everything is accurate. Schedule a start/end date or launch the election immediately to engage your voters without delay.

Vote

eVote ensures a seamless voting experience for your participants. With a single click, broadcast SMS and emails are sent to notify your voters about the election. They can conveniently record their votes through any internet-enabled device, making the process accessible and user-friendly.

Results

Once the election concludes, the approved election officer can generate the results. Our automated system securely tabulates the votes for accuracy and transparency. This additional security measure ensures the integrity of the outcome. You can choose to publish and share the results with your voters directly on your eVote website, fostering trust and participation.
